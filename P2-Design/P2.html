  • Client Name: Cattius Ladius Crazius, a.k.a "Crazy Cat Lady"
  • Website name: Cattius' Cat Castle
  • Contact Person: Cattius Ladius Crazius
  • Position in the "firm": CEO and Owner

About the Client

Cattius is a 67 years old lady that loves cats. She is a retired veterinarian that is alturistic in nature. During her career, she worked for 40 years in an animal shelter, mostly focused on domestic animals. Cattius wishes to remain active within the community even though retired, and feels that our website may help her achieve her goal of bringing animals, specifically cats, and people together.

3. Purpose and Goals

The website's purpose is twofold:

  1. Local level:
    • Provide a service to adopt and deliver homeless cats.
    • Place for stress release, by cuddling with the cats.
  2. Global level:
    • Deliver facts about cats to people around the world.
    • Entertainment for people, by cats.
    • Information about cats' caretaking for cat owners.
    • Make everybody love cats.

4. Audience

The users of this website can be categorized into the following:

  • People who love cats.
  • People who found a homeless cat and do not want it.
  • People want to adopt a cat.

7. Content of the Webstite

There will be nine separate pages of the website. Structure:

  • Home
  • Adopt
    • How to adopt
    • Our Cats
  • Hand in cats
  • Facts and Education
    • History
    • Anatomy
    • Cat care
  • Entertainment
  • About Us
    • About
    • Contact

Descriptions of the pages:

Home

Information about the shelter will be found on the about-page. Contact information such as address, email, phone number and opening hours will be presented here along with the history of the cat-home. A short biography of the owner Catius along with a short text about her motivation for helping homeless cats can be found here.

Adopt

Adopt will only provide a collapsible menu to reach the “How to adopt” and “Our cats” pages, and will therefore not have any content on its own.

How to Adopt

This page will have information about how you can adopt a cat from the home.
JavaScript form, fill out info about yourself.
Contact information, contact / address.
Direct link to gallery where the available cats are displayed.

Our Cats

Gallery of the cats currently at the Castle. Here you can browse the adoptable cats. The purpose of this page is to motivate people to adopt the cats.

The cat shelter will also have a social media account, on facebook, that shares pictures and stories of the cats.

A short text will be located next to the picture of the cat. Characteristics of the cat along with how and why the cat ended up at the home will be presented here.

Hand In

This page explains what to do if you have found a cat and want to hand it in to the shelter.

Facts and Education

This page has three subpages: Cat Care, Anatomy, and History.The user cannot click "Facts and Education", but must instead choose one subpage from the drop down.

Cat Care

This page will explain the many different aspects of taking care of a cat.

Anatomy

This page will give the user more facts about the cat's anatomy.

History

This page contains information about the history of the cat.

Entertainment

This page contains funny videos and other amusing stuff about cats < 3

About Us

This page has two subpages: About and Contact. The user cannot click "About us", but must instead choose one subpage from the drop down.

About

This page contains information about Cattius and her cat shelter.

Contact

Here, the user can find all contact information to the cat shelter. Opening hours, address, phone number and email will be displayed here.

8. Specific Preferences

Our client does not have many preferences when it comes to design of the webstie.

  • Each page on the Web Site should load within 2 seconds.
  • Each page must have at least one picture of a cat.
  • The site should be easy to navigate, such that people of the ages 7-80, who have some computer knowledge, can use it.
  • It should be possible to update images and information about the cats in the home. (This will be done by a technical supprt team if this function goes beyond the scope of this course.)
